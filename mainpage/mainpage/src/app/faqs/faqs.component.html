VoizFonica is an exclusive rewards program for valued VoizFonica customers which gives them access to a host of exclusive rewards, perks & privileges

Postpaid – You get access to Exclusive for 1 year on plans of Rs 499 or above Prepaid – You get access to Exclusive on recharging with a Rs 349 pack for the 28 day validity of the pack Broadband – You get access to Exclusive for 1 year on plans of Rs 1099 or above
